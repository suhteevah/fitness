## ABSOLUTE RULES

1. **NO PORK.** No bacon, no ham, no pork sausage, no pork chops, no pulled pork. Not even "lean pork." Not even if it's the only meat on sale. Jimmy Dean breakfast sausage is pork — it was flagged when Matt accidentally bought it.

2. **KETO.** <12g net carbs per day. No grains, no sugar (except in weekend pastry treats which are explicitly non-keto), no starchy vegetables, no fruit (except small amounts of berries occasionally).

3. **ANIMAL-BASED.** Primary calories from animal products: meat, eggs, cheese, butter, ghee. No seed oils. No plant-based protein substitutes.

4. **LOW GREASE.** Matt gets sick from high-grease meats. This means:
   - Chicken BREAST over thighs
   - Turkey sausage 93/7 over beef sausage
   - Sirloin over ribeye (when choosing lean)
   - Ground beef 90/10 minimum (not 80/20)
   - Add fat back via ghee/butter/cheese rather than greasy meat cuts
   - Drain excess grease when browning ground meat
