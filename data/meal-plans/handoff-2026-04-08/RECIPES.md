# RECIPES & COOKING INSTRUCTIONS
## Keto Meal Prep + Weekend Pastry

---

## MEAL PREP — SUNDAY PREP GUIDE (~1.5 hours)

### Step 1: Hard-Boil Eggs (15 min)
- Place 12-16 eggs in single layer in pot
- Cover with cold water by 1 inch
- Bring to rolling boil
- Turn off heat, cover, let sit 12 minutes
- Transfer to ice bath immediately
- Peel when cool, store in container

### Step 2: Cook Breakfast Protein (15 min)

**V5 — Ground Beef:**
- Brown 12 oz ground beef 90/10 in skillet over medium-high
- Season with smoked paprika + garlic powder + black pepper
- Drain any excess grease (low grease rule)
- Portion into 4 breakfast containers

**V6 — Turkey Sausage:**
- Cook 16 oz turkey sausage 93/7 in skillet
- Season with oregano + garlic powder
- Portion into 4 breakfast containers

### Step 3: Cook Lunch Protein (25 min)

**Chicken Breast (both V5 and V6):**
- Preheat oven to 375°F
- If breasts are thick, butterfly or pound to even thickness
- Season with onion powder, dried thyme, salt
- Bake 20-25 minutes until internal temp hits **165°F**
- Let rest 5 minutes before slicing
- Portion 8 oz per container into 4 lunch containers
- Add 2 oz gouda (V5) or havarti/provolone (V6) on top

**Turkey Burger Patties (V4 alternative):**
- Form 24 oz ground turkey into 4 × 6 oz patties
- Season with onion powder + dried thyme + salt
- Pan-fry or grill 4-5 min per side to 165°F
- Place in lunch containers with gouda melted on top

### Step 4: Prep Dinner Protein (20 min)

**Steak (V5 — chuck/NY strip/ribeye):**
- Pat dry with paper towels
- Season generously with coarse salt + cracked pepper + dried rosemary
- Heat cast iron or heavy pan to smoking hot
- Sear 3-4 min per side for medium (130-135°F internal)
- Rest 5 minutes before slicing
- Portion into 4 dinner containers with 2 oz blue cheese crumbles

**Sirloin (V6 — leanest cut):**
- Same method as above BUT:
- Sirloin is lean — DON'T OVERCOOK. Pull at 130-135°F.
- It gets tough fast past medium
- Hot pan, 3-4 min/side max
- Add extra ghee when cooking to compensate for leanness

### Step 5: Make Herb Butter (5 min)
- Mix softened butter with:
  - Minced fresh or dried rosemary
  - Garlic powder
  - Pinch of salt
- Roll in plastic wrap into a log
- Refrigerate — slice off rounds to put on dinner steaks

### Step 6: Portion Snacks (5 min)
- Beef sticks: grab-and-go, no prep needed
- Pre-bag macadamia nuts in 1 oz portions (small ziplock bags)
- Cheese sticks: grab-and-go

### Step 7: Assemble Containers
- **4 breakfast containers:** Scrambled eggs (cook fresh AM is fine), ground beef/turkey sausage, cheese
- **4 lunch containers:** Sliced chicken breast, cheese, 2 hard-boiled eggs
- **4 dinner containers:** Sliced steak, herb butter round, blue cheese/feta
- Snacks stay in their bags/packaging

## INDIVIDUAL COOKING METHODS

### Frozen Salmon Pan-Fry (from V2/V3)
1. Heat pan to medium-high with 1 tbsp ghee
2. Place frozen skin-on portions **skin-side UP**
3. Cook 4-5 minutes undisturbed
4. Flip to skin-side down
5. Cover with lid
6. Finish 6-10 minutes
7. Target internal temp: **145°F**
8. Result: crispy skin, flaky interior

### Chicken Breast Baking
- 375°F, 20-25 minutes
- Internal temp: **165°F** (USDA requirement for all chicken)
- Let rest 5 min before slicing
- Butterfly or pound thick breasts for even cooking

### Steak Searing
- Pat dry (moisture prevents sear)
- Smoking hot cast iron
- 3-4 min per side
- Don't touch while searing (let crust form)
- Pull at 130-135°F for medium
- Rest 5 minutes (carries over ~5°F)

### Safe Cooking Temperatures
| Protein | Internal Temp |
|---|---|
| Chicken (all cuts/ground) | 165°F |
| Ground beef/turkey | 160°F |
| Steak (medium) | 135°F |
| Steak (medium-rare) | 130°F |
| Salmon | 145°F |
| Eggs (hard-boiled) | 12 min covered off-heat |

## WEEKEND PASTRY RECIPES (Non-Keto Treat)

### Base: Rough Puff Pastry from Scratch

**Ingredients:**
- 2 cups all-purpose flour
- 1 cup (2 sticks) unsalted butter, cold, cut into ½-inch cubes
- 1 tsp salt
- 1 tsp sugar
- ½ cup ice water

**Method:**
1. Combine flour, salt, sugar in large bowl
2. Add cold butter cubes — toss to coat in flour
3. DO NOT fully cut in butter — leave chunky pea-sized pieces
4. Add ice water gradually, mix until dough barely comes together
5. Turn out onto floured surface
6. Roll into rectangle (~12×6 inches)
7. Fold in thirds (like a letter)
8. Rotate 90°, roll out again
9. Fold in thirds again
10. Wrap in plastic, refrigerate 30 min minimum
11. Repeat roll-fold-turn 2 more times (total 4 folds)
12. Final rest in fridge 30 min before use

### Recipe 1: Apple Cinnamon Turnovers
1. Roll dough to ¼-inch thick
2. Cut 5×5 inch squares
3. Dice 1 apple per 2 turnovers, toss with cinnamon + sugar + pinch nutmeg
4. Place filling on one half
5. Fold into triangle, press edges with fork to seal
6. Brush with egg wash (1 egg + 1 tbsp water)
7. Cut 2-3 small slits on top for steam
8. Bake 400°F, 20-25 minutes until golden
9. Cool 5-10 min on rack
10. Optional: dust with powdered sugar

### Recipe 2: Chocolate Pastry Pockets
1. Same rough puff dough
2. Roll out, cut 3×5 inch rectangles
3. Place 2-3 chocolate squares or 1 tbsp chips on one end
4. Fold over, press edges with fork
5. Egg wash, sprinkle sugar + cinnamon
6. Bake 400°F, 18-22 minutes
7. Cool 5 min — **MOLTEN inside, be careful**

### Recipe 3: Savory Cheese Twists
1. Roll dough into thin rectangle
2. Cover with shredded cheddar + garlic powder + pepper
3. Fold in half, press gently
4. Cut ½-inch strips
5. Twist each strip 3-4 times
6. Egg wash
7. Bake 400°F, 12-15 minutes until golden and crispy

- 2-3 Granny Smith or Honeycrisp apples
- 1 chocolate bar or bag dark chocolate chips
- Powdered sugar (optional)
- Cinnamon (if low)
- Already have: flour, butter, salt, sugar, eggs
