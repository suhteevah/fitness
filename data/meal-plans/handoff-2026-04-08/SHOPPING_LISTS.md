## SHOPPING TIPS

1. **Check the Safeway/Raley's app before entering the store.** Digital coupons on eggs, ground turkey, and steak are where the biggest savings are.
2. **Always check the manager's special/markdown bin** in the meat department. Chuck steak for 50% off is common.
3. **Buy in bulk when on sale:** eggs (3 dozen), chicken breast, steak.
4. **Freeze what you won't use in 3-4 days.** Especially if you buy heavy on meat.
5. **Safeway is generally slightly cheaper** than Raley's for eggs and chicken.
6. **One-time buys (spices, ghee, pecans, seasoning) last months.** First cycle is always the most expensive.
